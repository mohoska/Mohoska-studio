Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

This policy explains what data Mohoska Studio collects, why, and how it is protected. As a business operating within the European Union, this policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Hungarian data protection law.

1. Who We Are

This website is operated by Mohoska Studio, a freelance video editing business based in Hungary.

Data Controller: Mohoska Studio
Contact: gabcifoto@gmail.com
Country: Hungary, European Union

2. What Data We Collect

We only collect data that you provide voluntarily through our contact form:

  • Name — so we know who we are speaking with
  • Email address — so we can reply to your enquiry
  • Message content — the details of your project or question

We do not collect sensitive personal data, payment information, or any data from minors under 16 years of age without verifiable parental consent.

3. How We Collect Data

Data is collected only when you voluntarily submit the contact form on this website. Form submissions are processed by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form service. Formspree's own privacy policy applies to data in transit through their service. You can review it at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

4. Why We Process Your Data

Your data is processed for the following purpose and legal basis:

  • Purpose: To respond to your enquiry and discuss potential work together
  • Legal basis: Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) — responding to a direct business enquiry you initiated

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or automated decision-making.

5. How Long We Keep Your Data

Contact form submissions are retained only for as long as necessary to respond to and conclude your enquiry — typically no longer than 12 months. After this period, data is deleted.

6. Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services which may process data:

  • Formspree — processes contact form submissions. Data is transmitted securely to our email.
  • YouTube (Google LLC) — our showreel is embedded via YouTube. When you play the video, YouTube may set cookies and collect usage data under their own privacy policy. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's servers. Google may log your IP address. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Tag Manager / Analytics (planned) — this website may in future use Google Tag Manager to measure site performance and visitor statistics. When activated, this will be disclosed in this policy and your consent will be requested via our cookie banner before any tracking begins.

7. Cookies

This website currently uses only functional cookies necessary for the site to operate. These do not track you and do not require consent.

When Google Tag Manager / Analytics is activated in future, we will use analytical cookies. You will be asked for your consent via the cookie banner before any such cookies are set. You may withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

As an EU resident you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction — you can ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to object — you can object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at gabcifoto@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Hungarian data protection authority: Nemzeti Adatvédelmi és Információszabadság Hatóság (NAIH) at naih.hu.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data. Contact form submissions are transmitted over HTTPS. We do not store your data on this website's server — submissions go directly to our email via Formspree.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time, particularly when new services are added to this website. The date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent update. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
